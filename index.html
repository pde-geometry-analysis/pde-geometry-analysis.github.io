PDE in Geometry and Analysis

5th — 9th January 2026, at the ICMS in Edinburgh

Three mini-courses, given by Lucas Ambrozio, Alessandro Pigati, and Melanie Rupflin, targeted towards PhD students and post-docs. There will be eight speaking-slots to be filled by attendees.

Registration deadline: 1st August 2025 (at 2359 BST)

Registration and accommodation

Attendance is by invitation. To apply, please fill in this form. Accommodation, breakfast, lunch, and one conference dinner will be provided by the conference. We do not, at this time, have funding for travel or other dinners.
